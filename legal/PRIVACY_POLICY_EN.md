# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 19, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Flan ("the App") processes your personal information.

## 1. Data Collection and Storage

**The App does not operate servers and does not collect or store your personal information.**

All data (chat history, character settings, prompt configurations, API keys, etc.) is stored only on your device and will be deleted when you uninstall the app.

## 2. Third-Party Services

The App communicates directly with Google Gemini API using the API key you provide.

- The App only relays communication between you and the API, and does not store or collect data.
- Your API key and messages are sent directly from your device to Google.
- Data processing related to API usage is governed by [Google AI Terms of Service](https://policies.google.com/terms/generative-ai).

## 3. User Rights

Users can view or delete their data within the app at any time, and can completely remove all data by uninstalling the app.
